Lasagna

Description

This is considered the world's best lasagna. With 5 michelin stars.

Because tiremakers definitely know a lot about food.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Cloves Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic on medium heat.
  2. Season with spices.
  3. Boil lightly salted water.
  4. Cook noodles.
  5. Drain noodles and rinse.
  6. Combine ricotta cheese with egg and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  8. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  9. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  10. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  11. Repeat layers.
  12. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes.
  13. Allow it to cool down sufficiently.
